Bottled

Yum! Ice Mountain, Aquafina, Pure life, or Fiji.... But not Dasani that has never been water and will never be water. Make sure that the seal has not been broken, and do not refill the same water bottle to many times. After too many times of refilling the same water bottle, erosion sets in and the inner linings of the plastic get in the water.

Amount

"drinking 8-9 glasses of water a day" Listen, nobody drinks that much water. not even a dolphin! Newer research has found that actually, you're supposed to drink half your bodyweight in ounces of liquid. So water counts, but also juices count. Any sort of liquid adds up to the total ounces you need to reach.

Tap

Although many believe tap to be "worse" for you, there is no empirical evidence to back those claims up. In fact, tap water has to be federally filtered and constantly checked upon to make sure it's at the right levels, bottled water isn't held to the same standard and isn't take care of as precisely.
