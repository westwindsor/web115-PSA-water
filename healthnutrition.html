Breath

Drinking water can actually give you better breath! Bad breath is most commonly a result of bacteria that builds up in the mouth. The same way water at the bottom of a river erodes the ground, water in your mouth takes most of the buildup of bacteria and plaque and brings it into your stomach where bile breaks it down.

Hydration

Becoming dehydrated is a danger that too many people blow off like it can never happen to them. Dehydration can lead to sunstrokes, vomiting, and even diarrhea. So for the sake of everyone, and yourself, bring a water bottle with you on your next day out in the sun.

Sports Drinks

In the past, people have tried to prove that sports drinks like Powerade, Propel, and Gatorade are better for you than water. This can be true for high performance athlete that needs the extra edge for working out or performing in there given activity. However, if you aren't active stay away from the sugary drinks and stick to water.
