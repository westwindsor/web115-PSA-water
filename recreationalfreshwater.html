Swimming

The buddy system sounds cute and funny, but there's a reason people still use it. It works. No matter what age you are not having a lifeguard with you while swimming is dangerous. You never know when you could cramp up or injure yourself. Swim with a buddy.

Fishing

Whether you're fishing on a boat, or fishing on a pier, the fact of the matter is that you have a very sharp, barbed hook in your control. If you're not careful you'll end up hooking yourself and taking a not so fun trip to the hospital to have them stitch you up. Be careful where you're swinging that thing.

Wild Life

"Sharks are only in the ocean" as the Bull shark grins and licks his lips. Yes Bull sharks are freshwater sharks but there are plenty of other freshwater wildlife that are much more common, and thus much more dangerous. Fish like: Piranhas, electric eels, giant catfish, and even some Crocodiles.

Boating

There's nothing better on a summer day spending a day out on the lake with the family. Things like: fishing, swimming, and tubing. None of this can happen without a driver. Make sure you get the proper license from a official boating .gov cite to get certified.
