Before

Having a plan is important for a tsunami, however, it is less important than a hurricane or flood. The difference is if a tsunami is coming there is no time to prepare defense measures no time to grab valuables, you need to run and get to high ground as fast as possible.

During

Once the tsunami has hit and you are safely on high ground, stay there until authorities have informed you that it is safe to come back. The reason is that often tsunamis come in tidal waves, and there is usually never just one.

After

Essentially after a tsunami hits, you can treat the situation the same as a flood. Still wait for the signal of officials to tell you whether or not it is safe to return, but do not walk or drive in any still water due to electrical currents that may be exposed.
