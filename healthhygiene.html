Body

The dirt that builds up on your body over the course of a day is tremendous considering how little we can see it. Also, the amount of dead skin cells alone is interesting in and of itself, but the fact remains. After 24 hours you should be cleaning your body.

Hands

Wash your hands! Wash them all the time. Make sure that if you are washing them more than after bathroom visits and before food you are counter balancing that with using the right kind and amount of lotion so that your hands do not dry out.

Teeth

Interesting research is coming out, proposing the idea that flossing isn't actually as important that dentist have been saying it is. However, brushing your teeth will always be important. Every 12 hours is a good place to benchmark yourself at, twice a day. You can have serious problems like: cavities, gum disease, or even fractured teeth!

Hair

Previously stated you should be showering every day, however, washing your hair every day is not as big of a concern as one might think, actually, just the opposite. It's BAD if you wash your hair every day; every other day is more of an appropriate amount to stay clear of hair damage.
